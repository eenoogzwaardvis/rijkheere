## Projects
### Time-Series Analyis of Urban Development in the Balikpapan area, Indonesia
In this project, I investigated changes in NDVI values in Balikpapan after the announcement of the neighboring Ibu Kota Nusantara, Indonesia's new capital. The images show that NDVI time series can be used to detect where and when changes in the landscape occured. Even when they are not yet visible on satellite imagery.

### River Flow Velocity Estimation using UAV Imagery
In this project, I was challenged to find a way of estimating a river's flow velocity without the use of ground control points. The image shows a frame of a drone video, with a vector map indicating flow speeds at different places in the river.

### A Map of Southern Bohemia
Surely you can make a map, but can you make it informative and nice to look at? That was the challenge for this project. I was assigned Southern Bohemia (Jihočeský kraj), a beautiful region of the Czech Republic. I researched the region and made several maps introducing its characteristics. The set of maps was printed on A0 paper and made into a poster.
